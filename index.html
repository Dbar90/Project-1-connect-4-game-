About the Game:
Description and Objectives:
  • Be the first player to connect four of your corresponding 'chips' in a row.
  • Match your 'chips' horizontally.
  • Match your 'chips' vertically.
  • Match your 'chips' diagonally.
  • The player sitting on the left goes first!
How to play:
  • Each player will take a turn dropping their 'chips' into one of the seven columns.
  • To do this simply mouseover whichever column you wish to drop your 'chip' into.
  • Then click the button and your 'chip' will fall into the first available open spot of that column.
